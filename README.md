# Cineslate

**Your cinema slate, beautifully clear.**

Cineslate is a film and TV companion for iPhone and iPad. Browse what’s trending, dive into rich title pages, and keep your own lists—without ads, accounts, or noise.

Part of the [Sunpebble](https://github.com/sunpebble) family of thoughtful apps.

## Why Cineslate

- **Discover what’s on** — A cinematic home feed with hero picks, today’s buzz, genres, studios, and people to follow.
- **Your lists, your pace** — Watching now, up next, watch later, and history live on your device so you always know where you left off.
- **Title pages that feel like the movies** — Big artwork, synopses, cast, stills, and “more like this” when you’re deciding what to watch next.
- **Play from your library** — Optionally connect Plex and start playback from a title page when that show or film is already in your collection, with subtitle support when you need it.
- **Private by design** — No tracking, no ads. Your lists and preferences stay on your phone unless you choose to link an external service.
- **English & 简体中文** — Follows your system language.

## Who it’s for

You want a calm place to explore film and TV, save what matters to you, and—if you run your own media—jump straight into playback without juggling another cluttered app.
